4. How we store and share your data

4.1 Contact form submissions

Form data is transmitted via Google Apps Script to a private Google Sheet accessible only to Avyxon AI Labs personnel. Google processes this data as a sub-processor under Google's terms of service.

4.2 Analytics

Analytics data is stored on Google LLC servers, which may be located outside India and the European Economic Area. Google acts as a data processor under a Data Processing Agreement with us. Data retention is set to 14 months.

4.3 Other third parties

We use Cloudflare for email address obfuscation and CDN delivery. Cloudflare may process your IP address as part of standard CDN operation.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside India and the EU — principally the United States (Google's primary infrastructure). For EU residents, such transfers are covered by Google's Standard Contractual Clauses. For data subjects in India, such transfers are subject to applicable DPDP Act provisions.

7. Your rights

Rights under the DPDP Act 2023 (India)

  • Right to access — obtain a summary of your personal data we hold and the processing activities
  • Right to correction and erasure — have inaccurate data corrected or your data erased where processing is no longer necessary
  • Right to grievance redressal — raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see Section 9)
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to nominate — nominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity

Rights under GDPR (EU / EEA residents)

  • Right of access (Article 15) — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification (Article 16) — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (Article 17) — "right to be forgotten"
  • Right to restriction of processing (Article 18)
  • Right to data portability (Article 20)
  • Right to object (Article 21) — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., your national Data Protection Authority)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@avyxon.ai. We will respond within 30 days.
